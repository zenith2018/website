Zenith 2017

More than 2000 students from 16 ICSE and ISC schools across Mumbai participated on 6th and 7th October 2017 for LPHS' large-scale cultural and sports interschool competition –Zenith 2017. Zenith was the first festival of its kind to feature augmented reality in and across its events. 40 cultural events had been organized with the addition of new exciting events like ‘How To Get Away With Murder’ (murder mystery) , ‘Zenith Stock Exchange’ ‘Loop The Hoop' (glow in the dark basketball) ‘Musical.ly’, Hindi Extempore , Culinary events , News presenting , JAM sessions along with band, quiz and startup pitching events. For the first time, personality contest was being held with 5 exciting and extensive rounds and Mst. Shaan Bhatt of Bombay Scottish , Mahim and Ms. Inika Khosla from Arya Vidya Mandir were declared as Mr. Zenith and Ms Zenith respectively. 20 sports events were organized at St. Andrews Bandra football turf and Bandra YMCA. Girls and boys cricket under arm tournament was organized for the first time as well as football and basket ball tournaments. An exclusive Instagram page for the event was set up with mannequin challenges for schools to showcase their preparation for the event. As LPHS was a non participant school, It was an excellent opportunity for more than 300 students from ICSE and ISC to actively organize and understand the dynamics of each event. St. Gregorios high school , Chembur and Hiranandani foundation school , Thane were declared as the overall champions for ICSE and ISC events respectively.
Competitions on gaming, art, drama, (you name it and we had it!) had determined bstudents from over twelve ISC schools eager to showcase their skills and abilities. These young artists performed spectacularly in every cultural event held in school and showcased their teamwork, ability and sportsmanship on the field.
To be at your Zenith, is to be at your pinnacle, your crowing point, and that’s what we at Lilavaibai Podar High School wish to achieve. Zenith 2017 set an incredibly high bar for all events to follow, and we couldn't be more excited for its subsequent edition to reach new heights!
