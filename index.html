Mission

A platform that recognizes creativity and passion, Zenith seeks to provide new avenues for students to accomplish deeds worthy of their calibre. At Zenith we believe that an extraordinary student goes beyond the boundaries of academic excellence by showcasing their dynamism in various fields. Our annual festival aims to bring these talents to light; to promote, nurture, and enhance the budding capabilities and skills of students.

Motto

“Grandeur can be appreciated best from its apex.”
To an onlooker, the mountain peak remains unseen. To one at the base, beheld is but overwhelming enormity. To a hiker, his appreciation is limited to his own climb. However, at the peak, we observe and appreciate the true glory of the mountain, in its entirety. Thus, we bring to you Zenith, so that the beauty extending far and beyond the usual horizons of your vision is uncovered in the best of light.

About Lilavatibai Podar High School

Lilavatibai Podar is an English-medium, co-educational, independent institution founded in 1927 by Late Shri Ganesh Narayan Podar. The school strives to impart sound, liberal education aimed at the holistic development of its students. Since its inception, LPHS has reached great heights with the collective effort of the management, heads and staff under the guidance of our honourable principal, Mrs. Norina Fernandes, who has always been our guiding light, It has been successful in its mission of creating global citizens year after year

Ms. Norina Fernandes

Brian Ferren once said "Visionaries not only believe the impossible can be done, but that it must be done." The truth in this is brought out constantly by the captain of our ship, our principal Ms Norina Fernandes. It is because of her exemplary vision that Lilavatibai Podar pushes the boundaries the way it does today, with a platform for its students to express themselves freely. She embodies a beacon of light and inspiration that helps us shine, and for that, we are eternally grateful.
