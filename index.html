MOTTO
“Grandeur can be appreciated best from its apex.”
To an onlooker, the mountain peak remains unseen. To one at the base, beheld is but overwhelming enormity. To a hiker, his appreciation is limited to his own climb. However, at the peak, we observe and appreciate the true glory of the mountain, in its entirety. Thus, we bring to you Zenith, so that the beauty extending far and beyond the usual horizons of your vision is uncovered in the best of light.

MISSION
A platform that recognizes creativity and passion, Zenith seeks to provide new avenues for students to accomplish deeds worthy of their calibre. At Zenith we believe that an extraordinary student goes beyond the boundaries of academic excellence by showcasing their dynamism in various fields. Our annual festival aims to bring these talents to light; to promote, nurture, and enhance the budding capabilities and skills of students.
