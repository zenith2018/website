Our Cause

Mental Health awareness

Zenith is a platform for students like us to express ourselves, to showcase what we as a generation stand for. We are moving towards the doors of the real world at a pace much faster than anyone can fathom and we all know, in our heart of hearts, that we want to change that world for the better. We want to be more aware, more accepting, more understanding and in all, be better people. Of all the movements that we’ve so vehemently supported, is that of mental health awareness something so simple, so vital and understandably ordinary, yet something that has been ignored for so very long.

Mental health is the simple well-being of one’s mind. Unfortunately, given the tremendous stigma around mental illness, mental health suffered a severe blow. This aggravated on account societal pressures, flawed norms and various biases against simple human existence and expression. Today, 7.5% of India's population suffers from at least one mental illness, making it a problem which needs immediate attention, mental health awareness.

On an individual level, it's important to start conversations, spread awareness and encourage constructive discourse to tackle this issue as efficiently as possible. Lilavatibai Podar is thus, proud to announce that Zenith 2018 will be advocating mental health awareness.

We at Zenith strongly believe that when expression of individuality is suppressed, it becomes impossible to identify with your surroundings, and we have tried our best to make sure every talent finds a platform for expression. We hope to provide a stage secure and accepting enough for you to hone your talents and skills, ignite that light within each of your hearts so that you can watch as the darkness around you dissipates by the means of your own efforts. Hopefully through this talent fest we can come together, with the unflinching, unapologetic and uninhibited spirit of our own diverse unity to make this world a better, more interesting place. 

About Lilavatibai Podar High School

Lilavatibai Podar is an English-medium, co-educational, independent institution founded in 1927 by Late Shri Ganesh Narayan Podar. The school strives to impart sound, liberal education aimed at the holistic development of its students. Since its inception, LPHS has reached great heights with the collective effort of the management, heads and staff under the guidance of our honourable principal, Mrs. Norina Fernandes, who has always been our guiding light, It has been successful in its mission of creating global citizens year after year
