Contingent Leaders Meet

The Contingent Leaders Meet happend on the 18th of September at Lilavatibai Podar High School. Couldn't attend the meet? Catch up here!

"Great is the art of ending, but greater is the art of beginning"
- Chinese proverb
On September 18th, 2018, Team Zenith reached the first milestone of our journey- the Contingent Leaders' Meet. It was attended by 16 schools, namely Podar ISC Nerul, Arya Vidya Mandir (Bandra West, Bandra East and Juhu branches), Hiranandani Foundation School (Thane and Powai branches), Children's Academy Malad, St. John's Universal School, City International Scool, The Scholar High School, CNM School, Gundecha Education Academy, Activity High School, Jasudben M.L. School, Hasanat High School and N.L Dalmia High School.
The function was hosted by the ICSE Head Boy and Girl, Vinayak Bhattacharya and Marishka D'Gama. Dignitaries in attendance included Ms. Sheila Alexander, the Vice Principal of LPHS, and Mr. Zaheer Sareea, the Headmaster of the ISC section. It was initiated by a soulful prayer and hymn performed by the music team, which was well received by the audience. This was followed by an address from the Chairpersons, Anish Sudhan and Akanksha Agrawal, who gave a powerful speech about the inimitable spirit of Zenith and its theme, mental health awareness.
After this, the crux of the event began- the general rules and registration rules were explained by Rajveer Veera and Ashna Agarwal, our Vice Chairpersons. Special rules for sports were enumerated by Anish. Following this, our category heads explained the details for their respective fields of expertise- Conventional Sports was done by Tirna Roy and Param Kothari; Unconventional Sports by Mallika Parihar and Siddharth Bharadwaj; Informals by Sama Rajput and Pari Baghar; Literary Arts by Rutuparna Deshpande and Shreya Khobragade; Visual Arts by Aira Shetty and Krisha Tahiliani; Performing Arts by Prarthna Iyer and Akanksha Datta Gupta; and finally, Youniverse by Aashna Shah and Marishka D'Gama. After each category, a question and answer session took place to clarify doubts.
Lilavatibai Podar has always believed in giving a stage to all talents, which is why the Contingent Leaders' Meet also included a musical medley and a dance performance related to our theme which left the audience enthralled. Spoken word poet and former student Aranya Johar, whose first video went viral online, shared an amazing piece spreading awareness about social anxiety. Divij Pritamani, the Chairperson of Zenith 2017, addressed the crowd and left us on the edge of our seats.
After a quick session of distribution of themes and reiteration of registration rules, the Meet came to an end with a teacher from CNM School sharing her positive experience. We wholeheartedly agree with her- the experience was truly like no other, and we cannot contain our excitement for the fete! 

- Shreya Khobragade
